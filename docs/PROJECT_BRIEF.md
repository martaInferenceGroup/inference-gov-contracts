# Project Brief: Automated Government Contract Discovery & Application Support

## Overview

Reduce manual effort in finding government contract opportunities and speed up tender submission by automating key parts of the workflow — from discovery through to application drafting. Claude is used for relevance filtering and content generation.

## Aims & Goals

1. **Discover** — Automatically scrape Contracts Finder and Find a Tender on a scheduled basis (e.g. every Friday morning)
2. **Store & Organise** — Centralise all scraped opportunities for review, filtering, and prioritisation
3. **Filter for Relevance** — Use Claude to assess fit based on sector, value, eligibility, geography, deadlines
4. **Support Applications** — Use Claude to draft answers to common tender questions using reusable content
5. **Build a Knowledge Base** — Maintain a library of company information, case studies, credentials, policies, and pre-written responses for Claude to draw on
6. **Automate the Workflow** — Define how tenders move from scraped data → shortlist → review → drafting → submission
